the specific heat  at constant pressure is 
$C_p=\left(\partial{h}/\partial{T}\right)_p$,
then:

\begin{equation}
\begin{split}

\left(\sum_i h_i\frac{\partial{Y_i}}{\partial{T}}\right)_p
\end{split}
\end{equation}
